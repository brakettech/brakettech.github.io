Service

Braket Solar Alerts is a hobbyist, personal-use service that sends SMS notifications to the system owner about residential solar PV production anomalies.

Eligibility / Intended Use

The service is exclusively for the phone number owner receiving alerts and is not publicly offered or a commercial product. This is a personal notification tool for monitoring a single residential solar installation.

Message Types & Frequency

The system sends three notification categories:

  • Offline/No Data Alerts — Notifications when the solar monitoring system appears offline or stops reporting data
  • No Production Notifications — Alerts when solar panels produce near-zero energy for an extended period
  • Low Production Warnings — Alerts at yellow or red severity levels when system output falls significantly below expected levels based on weather conditions

Users typically receive approximately 10 messages monthly, though frequency varies with system activity and performance anomalies detected.

No Marketing

This service restricts communications to system alerts only. No promotional or marketing content will be sent. All messages relate exclusively to solar system performance monitoring.

Opt-Out / Help

Users can manage their subscription at any time:

  • Text STOP to unsubscribe from all alerts
  • Text START to reactivate alerts after unsubscribing
  • Text HELP for support information

You will receive a confirmation message after opting out. For additional assistance, contact scott@brakettech.com.
